About ParkiSense Voice Test

ParkiSense voice analysis works by examining vocal frequency stability. A Random Forest classifier trained on the PC-GITA dataset extracts vocal biomarkers (Jitter, Shimmer, Harmonicity) from an uploaded .wav file to screen for Parkinson's Disease early.

This tool is for screening purposes only and does not replace a professional neurological evaluation.

Audio Instructions
1
Ensure you are in a quiet room with minimal background noise.
2
Record a sustained vowel sound like 'ahhh' or 'oooh' for 3-5 seconds.
3
Save the recording as a WAV format audio file.
4
Upload the file using the secure upload zone.
5
Click Analyse Voice Sample. Our AI model will extract vocal biomarkers.
This is a screening tool only. Results are not a clinical diagnosis.
